What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is the permanent removal of trees to make room for something besides forest.Sometimes trees are removed for agriculture, construction or manufacturing. One of the most dangerous and unsettling effects of deforestation is the loss of animal and plant species due to their loss of habitat.In addition to the loss of habitat, the lack of trees also allows a greater amount of greenhouse gases to be released into the atmosphere.
