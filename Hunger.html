World Hunger

  • Hunger impacts one out of every nine people on earth
  • The Central African Republic is the world’s hungriest country as of 2018
  • Some causes of world hunger are poverty, food shortages, war & conflict, climate change, food waste, gender inequality, and forced migration.
  • Hunger affects people’s health and education.
  • It makes their body weak and vulnerable to diseases and infections as the body does not have the fuel to build muscle and fight off infections.
  • Without proper food, the brain isn’t able to develop properly.
  • Hunger also makes it difficult to concentrate on anything, especially for children.
  • Some ways to help solve world hunger can be food donations, sustainable farming, government intervention, climate resilience, and raise awareness.
