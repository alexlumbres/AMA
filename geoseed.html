What is GeoSeed?

GeoSeed is a type of bomb that contain seeds and/or a tree. For the bomb to work, it must first be carried by a plane, where the plane would fly to a certain altitude. When the plane has reached its altitude, the plane then drops the bomb, striking straight to the ground and essentially being planted in to the ground. Geoseed's purpose is to create an effecient way of planting seeds and/or trees to the ground without manually doing it by a person or machine.


How is this beneficial?

GeoSeed is a beneficial way to efficiently plant trees or seeds. The bomb isn't limited by the type of plant or tree is being placed into. This allows the bomb to have variety of plants to be planted. It can essentially help the people who either lack vegitation or greenery.
